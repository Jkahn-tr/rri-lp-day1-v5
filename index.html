Foundation and Vision

Dear Friends,

Welcome to Love and Passion: Our Ultimate Relationship Program. It is a privilege to be with you all once again, or possibly for the first time. We admire your willing hearts and respect the hunger and dedication to spend a week of our lives together all in the name of LOVE.

May we ignite our hearts, open our minds, engage in playful curiosity, laugh, enjoy each other's company, and allow grace-filled tears to flow. May we remember and forget in the same breath. May we notice what we have missed, and may we awaken to the love within us and all around us. In this space, we'll be diving deep enough to both lose ourselves and discover our true nature all at once.

This week, we invite you to decide to set expectations aside and step into the beauty of the reality of this experience we share here now. Let's jump heart first into all that we don't know, so we can emerge with the clarity to see our loved ones most accurately. After all, knowing unconditional love in this life is a gift precious beyond all comprehension.

We are all being called to love at the deepest level, let's take this journey together and discover something even more beautiful and magnificent than we have ever known before.

Thank you, we love you and are so grateful to share this with you.

The Seasons of Relationship

Every relationship moves through seasons. The wisdom isn't avoiding them - it's learning to navigate each one.

🌱 Spring

Springtime in the beginning - is it hard or easy to grow? Incredibly easy. If you start in springtime, you think you're a genius. Everything comes effortlessly, like bam - springtime. Growth happens almost without effort.

☀️ Summer - Testing Time

What always follows spring is a testing time. Summer is hot, it's a challenge. All of a sudden the relationship doesn't feel effortless anymore. You get tested, and some plants don't make it - they don't get enough nurturing, enough support.

🍂 Fall

If you make it through the testing time of summer, the universe seems to give us this cool season called fall - a reaping time where all of a sudden everything has the most magnificent colors, where everything is easy, fruit is available. It's all there.

❄️ Winter

Winter is what makes us stronger. Winter is what bonds us deepest in our relationships - those times where we came together when everything else looked like it was falling apart. It bonded us. Winter is where you learn to build a great fire and be close to the one you love.

The Theme of the Weekend

The Generous Lover

If there's a theme of this program that will get you through all the seasons, it's you deciding that you are the source of the love in your relationship. As long as you're trying to get it from somebody, you're operating out of scarcity - and scarcity never feels good. Doesn't feel good to you, doesn't feel good to the other.

Our theme for the whole week is to be a generous lover. What would a totally generous lover do for the one that they adore, for their beloved? What would they do?

In the beginning, almost everybody's a generous lover. But somewhere between seven hours and 70 years, the rules change. If you're a generous lover, the whole game changes. What if that's all you did - you just decided the love that I'm going to experience is the love that I'm giving?

If you're sincerely generous in your love of this person, willing to give it openly and evenly, not measuring what you're getting in return - it'll change everything. That's the first theme we're going to carry all week.
